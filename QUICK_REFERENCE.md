## ğŸ“ˆ Key Metrics to Track

### Website (Google Analytics)
- **Daily visitors** - Aim for 10% weekly growth
- **Bounce rate** - Under 60% is good
- **Avg. session duration** - Over 2 minutes is good
- **Conversion rate** - Email signups / visitors (aim for 2-5%)

### Email (Mailchimp)
- **Total subscribers** - Growth goal: +50/month initially
- **Open rate** - Industry avg: 20-25%, aim for 30%+
- **Click rate** - Industry avg: 2-3%, aim for 5%+
- **Unsubscribe rate** - Keep under 1%

### Content (Social)
- **Twitter followers** - Consistency matters more than speed
- **YouTube subscribers** - Quality over quantity
- **Engagement rate** - Comments + shares = good sign

## ğŸš¨ Troubleshooting

### "Email form not working"
**Check:**
1. Did you update Formspree form ID in `script.js`?
2. Did you deploy the updated files?
3. Check browser console for errors (F12)

### "Analytics showing no data"
**Check:**
1. Did you replace YOUR_GA_ID with actual ID?
2. Wait 24-48 hours for data to appear
3. Visit your own site to test (use incognito mode)

### "Website not loading"
**Check:**
1. Are files uploaded to hosting?
2. Is DNS configured correctly (if using custom domain)?
3. Check hosting platform status
